Seal User Manual (customer and Registered customer)

Log In

To avail the services of Seal, the admin should log in to the system. Click on Manage Your Seal Account. Two options will be shown: Customer and Admin. Click on the Admin option.

View Users

Admin will login to the system to view user and their details .Click on users to view users and admin can update users information if required.

View Inventory

Click on Inventory to view products and their price.

Inventory Status

Click on status to view inventory status of user in details.

Add Information

Click on Add New to add new user and new product

Add New User

Click on Add below Add New User and fill up the form to create new user.

Add New Product

Click on Add below Add New Product and fill up the form to insert new product.

View Profile

Click on profile icon at the top of the page to view profile.

Edit Information

Admins can update their general information by filling up the following form.

View Purchase History

Click on Log to view purchase history of users

View Report

Click on Report to view percentage of male and female employee.

Sign Out

After completing the application process log out of the account. Click on arrow icon beside profile icon to sign out of the system.
